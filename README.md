PROJECT CASE STUDY
Project Name: BlendGo
Project Type: Single Product Shopify Store
Platform: Shopify
Theme: Dawn
________________________________________
1.	Project Introduction
BlendGo is a single-product ecommerce store designed to sell a portable blender for people who want to maintain a healthy lifestyle while on the go.
2.	Project Objective
The main objective was to build a high-converting Shopify store focused on one product, with a clean landing page structure that encourages users to quickly understand the value and make a purchase.
3.	Target Audience
• Fitness enthusiasts
• Busy professionals
• Students
• Travelers
• People interested in healthy lifestyles
4.	Market Opportunity
Portable kitchen gadgets have grown in popularity due to increasing demand for convenience and healthy living.
5.	Problem Identification
Many people struggle to prepare healthy drinks outside their homes due to time constraints and lack of portable equipment.
6.	Product Solution
BlendGo solves this by offering a compact blender capable of making smoothies within seconds.
7.	Brand Strategy
BlendGo was positioned as a healthy lifestyle brand focused on convenience and energy.
8.	Brand Identity
Brand Personality:
Healthy – Energetic – Modern – Practical
9.	Visual Identity
Primary Color: #22C55E
Secondary Color: #111111
Accent Color: #F97316
Background: #FFFFFF
10.	Logo Concept
The logo combines a blender icon with the text BlendGo to represent convenience and mobility.
11.	Store Architecture
Landing page structured to guide the user through a purchase journey.
12.	Sections include:
Hero
Problem
Solution
Benefits
How it Works
Reviews
FAQ
CTA
13.	Hero Section Strategy
Headline: Blend Fresh Smoothies Anywhere
Purpose: Immediately communicate product benefit.
14.	Product Storytelling
The page explains the product through real-life use cases such as gym, office, and travel.
15.	Conversion Optimization
The layout focuses on clear calls to action and benefit-driven messaging.
16.	User Experience Design
Mobile-first layout
Fast navigation
Clear product information
17.	Shopify Tools Used
Platform: Shopify
Theme: Dawn
18.	Apps Implemented
Reviews App
Sticky Add To Cart
Trust Badges
Upsell App
19.	Visual Content Strategy
Lifestyle imagery and product demonstrations were used to increase customer trust.
20.	Project Outcome
The final result is a clean, modern single-product store designed to maximize conversions.
21.	
• Effective storytelling increases engagement
• Minimal design improves usability
• Strong CTAs improve conversion rates
